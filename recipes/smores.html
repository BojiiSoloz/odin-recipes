Smores Recipe

What are Smores?

Smores is a delicous snack that helps create lasting memories. This yummy treat is commonly eaten around the campfire, but tastes just as great out of the oven!

Ingredients

  • Graham Crackers
  • Chocolate
  • Marshmellows (Preferably the big ones)
  • Additional add-ons:
    • Strawberries
    • Bon Bons
    • Carmel
    • Bacon
    • Peanut Butter

Although it is not an ingredient, you will need a skewer in order to hold the smores over the fire.

Steps

I recommned starting by organizing all of the treats on a plate beforehand. That way once you are ready to begin, you will have access to everything.

  1. Stick your marshmellow with a skewer
  2. Roast the marshmellow until it is golden brown, or once the marshmellow has become gooey
  3. Place the marshmellow on your graham crackers and begin styling it to your liking!
  4. Eat and enjoy your creation!
